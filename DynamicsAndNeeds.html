Relationship Dynamics & Needs Assessment

A tool for exploring shared experiences and understanding underlying needs without judgment

Setting the Stage

This assessment focuses on dynamics and unmet needs rather than individual judgments. The goal is to create a safe space for open feedback, making our shared experience the object of attention and work. This isn't about assigning blame, but about genuinely understanding what might be contributing to feelings of "stuckness" and what we might collectively need to move forward.

1Reflective Questions on Current Dynamics

These open-ended questions encourage reflection on shared experience and underlying needs.

When you think about our current situation, what specific aspects feel most stuck or unchangeable?

If we could wave a magic wand, what's one area where you'd most want to see things move forward or shift?

What emotions or frustrations come up for you when you feel this sense of being "stuck"?

What do you believe might be missing or unaddressed that contributes to this feeling of inertia?

If we were able to address those missing pieces, what do you imagine would be different?

What's one small step you think we could take to start identifying or meeting an important need that's currently unmet?

2Needs-Focused Assessment

Rate how well these fundamental needs are being met in our current dynamic (1 = not met at all, 5 = fully met).

Sense of Security/Safety (emotional or practical)

Do you feel a stable foundation, knowing what to expect, and that your well-being is considered?

Understanding/Being Heard

Do you feel that your perspective is genuinely listened to and understood, even if there's disagreement?

Autonomy/Control

Do you feel you have appropriate agency or influence over decisions that affect you?

Connection/Belonging

Do you feel connected and that you belong, with shared purpose or support?

Recognition/Appreciation

Do you feel your contributions, efforts, or presence are seen and valued?

Clarity/Direction

Do you feel there's a clear understanding of goals, roles, or expectations?

Support/Help

Do you feel adequate support is available when needed?
